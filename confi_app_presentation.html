Process

Simple. Private. Powerful.

From thought to confession in three taps — always anonymous.

1

Write freely

Type whatever's on your mind. No name, no profile, no history attached. Just your words.

2

Release it

Your confession is encrypted, stripped of metadata, and floated into the community feed.

3

Connect anonymously

Others react, relate, and respond — all without knowing who you are. You stay safe.

4

Feel lighter

The act of confessing is cathartic. Thousands of others have felt exactly what you feel.

Features

Built for radical anonymity

🔐

Zero identity tracking

No account required. No email. No phone number. Your identity is never stored on our servers.

🫧

Floating confession bubbles

Confessions appear as beautiful 3D bubbles. Browse anonymously, tap to read, swipe to move on.

🤝

Anonymous reactions

Relate, support, or just send a "me too" — all without revealing who you are. Community without identity.

🛡️

End-to-end encryption

Every confession is encrypted in transit and at rest. Not even we can read your messages post-submission.

⏱️

Auto-expiring confessions

Set your confession to vanish after 24h, 7 days, or a custom time. Leave no permanent trace.

🌙

Dark mode native

Designed from the ground up for late-night honesty sessions. Soft on the eyes, heavy on the truth.
